The Philippines is a country in Southeast Asia best known for the beautiful scenery, especially the beaches.

Here are some top destinations in the Philippines

Boracay

  • is a resort island in the Western Visayas region.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: Puka Shell Beach

Puerto Princesa

  • is the capital and major flight gateway in Palawan.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: the Underground River

Baguio

  • Baguio City offers a cool climate, urban comforts, etc.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: Burnham Park (go karts, boats, etc)

Cebu

  • is the no.1 most visited tourist destination.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: sites (waterfalls, beaches, etc.)

Tagaytay

  • is a popular vacation spot near Manila.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: Taal Volcano

Bohol

  • is known for The Chocolate Hills, the bug-eyed Tarsier, etc.
  • Recommended place/s to visit: Chocolate Hills
